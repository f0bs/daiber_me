The 2019 Challenge

in cooperation with Major League Baseball

"How can MLB bring a beloved in-stadium experience, and create the same excitement and personalized touch online"

The Idea

We started by analyzing mlb.com customer data and conducting a series of user interviews. We found that live video was a great segment to bring the in-stadium experience online. So, we redesigned the MLB.tv experience into a social, interactive experience centered around friends, family and fellow fans.

The Framework

We focused on 3 main objectives to enhance the MLB experience

Personal Connection

Social Interaction

Continous Engagement

The Redesigned Experience

Personal Connection

Personalize the experience by displaying the content most relevant to each individual user. 

Bring new fans to the online experience with a dual-sided referral program.

Social Interaction

Empower social interaction by including chat features and virtual reactions to interact with other fans in real-time. 

Inspire social expression with Social Replay™ allowing fans to create and share their own short commented highlight videos.

Continuous Engagement

Introduce group gamification leveraging competitive drive to enhance fan excitement.

MLB rewards for loyal fans with a focus on limited merchandise and exclusive experiences.
